What is BA LL.B?

BA LL.B is an integrated undergraduate law program that combines Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Law degrees. This 5-year course is designed for students who aspire to build a career in the legal field right after their 12th grade.

The program provides a strong foundation in both arts subjects and legal studies, making it one of the most sought-after courses for law aspirants in India.

  • Duration: 5 Years (10 Semesters)
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with minimum 45-50% marks
  • Age Limit: Generally 20-22 years (varies by university)
  • Mode: Full-time Regular Program
  • Degree Type: Integrated Undergraduate Program
  • Recognition: Approved by Bar Council of India (BCI)

Eligibility Criteria

Check if you qualify for BA LL.B admission

Educational Qualification

  • 10+2 from recognized board
  • Minimum 45-50% aggregate marks
  • 40% for SC/ST candidates
  • 42% for OBC candidates
  • Valid in any stream (Science/Commerce/Arts)
  • English as compulsory subject

Age Requirements

  • Maximum 20 years for General category
  • 22 years for SC/ST/OBC
  • As on date of admission
  • Relaxation for PWD candidates
  • No upper age limit for some universities
  • Age verification required

Entrance Exams

  • CLAT (Common Law Admission Test)
  • AILET (All India Law Entrance Test)
  • LSAT India (Law School Admission Test)
  • SLAT (Symbiosis Law Admission Test)
  • University-specific entrance tests
  • Direct admission in private colleges

Special Provisions

  • NRI/NRI Sponsored Quota: 15% seats in private universities
  • Management Quota: Available in selected colleges
  • Foreign Nationals: Separate admission process
  • Sports Quota: Extra-curricular achievements considered
