Admission Process

Simple and transparent admission procedure

1

Get Information

Contact our counselors or download brochure to get detailed program information

2

Submit Application

Fill the application form online or at study center with required documents

3

Fee Payment

Pay program fees through online payment, DD, or at study center

4

Start Learning

Receive study materials and get access to LMS to begin your journey

Eligibility Criteria

  • For UG: 10+2 from recognized board
  • For PG: Bachelor's degree in relevant field
  • Minimum 40% marks in qualifying exam
  • No entrance exam required
  • Relaxation for reserved categories

Documents Required

  • 10th & 12th Mark Sheets
  • Graduation Certificates (PG)
  • ID Proof (Aadhar/PAN/Voter)
  • Passport Size Photos
  • Address Proof
  • Signature

Important Dates

  • Admission Start: January & July
  • Last Date: 31st March / 30th Sep
  • Document Verification: 7 days
  • Fee Payment: 15 days
  • Session Start: April / October
