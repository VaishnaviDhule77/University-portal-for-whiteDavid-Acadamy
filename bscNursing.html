Eligibility Criteria

Requirements for BSc Nursing Admission

Educational Requirements

  • 10+2 Passed: Minimum 45% Marks
  • Science Stream: PCB Mandatory
  • English: Core Subject
  • Age Limit: 17-35 Years
  • Relaxation: 5% for SC/ST
  • Minimum Age: 17 Years on 31st Dec

Medical Fitness

  • Physical Fitness: Required
  • Medical Certificate: Mandatory
  • Vision: 6/6 Normal
  • No Chronic Disease: Must be Fit
  • Mental Health: Sound Mind
  • Vaccination: Complete Records

Admission Process

  • Entrance Exam: State/National Level
  • Merit List: Based on Marks
  • Counseling: Seat Allotment
  • Document Verification: Required
  • Fee Payment: Confirmation
  • Reporting: Join College

Required Documents

  • 10th Mark Sheet & Certificate
  • 12th Mark Sheet & Certificate
  • Transfer Certificate
  • Character Certificate
  • Medical Fitness Certificate
  • Migration Certificate (if applicable)
  • Domicile Certificate
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport Size Photos (8-10)
  • Aadhar Card
