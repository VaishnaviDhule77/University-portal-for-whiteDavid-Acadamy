Admission Process

Simple & Transparent Admission Procedure

1

Application

Fill online application form and submit required documents

2

Entrance Exam

Appear for university entrance exam or submit national scores

3

Counseling

Attend counseling session for seat allocation and course selection

4

Fee Payment

Pay admission fee to confirm seat and complete enrollment

Eligibility Criteria

  • For B.Tech: 60% in 12th PCM
  • For BBA/BCA: 50% in 12th
  • For MBA: Graduation with 50%
  • Valid JEE/CLAT/MAT scores accepted
  • Relaxation for reserved categories

Documents Required

  • 10th & 12th Mark Sheets
  • Graduation Certificates (PG)
  • Entrance Exam Score Card
  • ID Proof (Aadhar/PAN)
  • Passport Size Photos
  • Migration Certificate

Important Dates

  • Application Start: March
  • Entrance Exam: May
  • Result Declaration: June
  • Counseling: July
  • Session Start: August
