Vibrant Campus Life

Where Learning Meets Fun and Friendship

Modern Hostels

Comfortable AC & Non-AC rooms with 24/7 security, Wi-Fi, mess facilities, common rooms, gym, and dedicated study areas. Separate hostels for boys and girls with wardens.

50+ Student Clubs

Technical clubs (Coding, Robotics, AI), Cultural clubs (Music, Dance, Drama), Sports clubs, Literary club, Photography club, Entrepreneurship cell, and many more for holistic development.

Annual Fest - VGU Udaan

3-day grand cultural and technical festival featuring celebrity performances, competitions, workshops, food carnival, and night concerts with 10,000+ participants.

Sports & Athletics

Inter-college tournaments, cricket league, football championship, basketball tournament, annual sports meet, yoga sessions, and adventure sports with professional coaching.

Food & Dining

Multiple food courts serving North Indian, South Indian, Chinese, Continental cuisines. Cafeteria, coffee shop, juice bar, and 24/7 tuck shop with hygienic and affordable food options.

Green Campus

30-acre lush green campus with gardens, walking tracks, eco-friendly initiatives, solar panels, rainwater harvesting, and peaceful environment perfect for learning and growth.
