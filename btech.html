What is B.Tech?

Understanding Bachelor of Technology Program

Program Overview

B.Tech (Bachelor of Technology) is a 4-year undergraduate engineering degree program that focuses on theoretical knowledge and practical application of technology and engineering principles.

Duration

4 Years (8 Semesters) - Full-time program with industry internships and practical training in final year.

Degree Type

Professional undergraduate degree recognized globally. Equivalent to B.E. (Bachelor of Engineering) in India.

Program Structure

  • Year 1: Basic Sciences & Engineering Fundamentals
  • Year 2: Core Engineering Subjects
  • Year 3: Specialization & Electives
  • Year 4: Projects & Industrial Training

Program Outcomes

  • Technical Skills: Problem-solving abilities
  • Practical Knowledge: Hands-on experience
  • Industry Ready: Placement opportunities
  • Higher Studies: M.Tech/MS/MBA eligibility

B.Tech Eligibility Criteria

Requirements for Engineering Admission

Academic Qualification

  • 10+2 or equivalent from recognized board
  • Physics, Chemistry & Mathematics compulsory
  • Minimum 45% marks (40% for reserved categories)
  • English as one of the subjects

Age Limit

  • Minimum age: 17 years as of Dec 31
  • Maximum age: 25 years (30 for reserved)
  • Age relaxation for SC/ST/OBC
  • No age limit for NRI/Foreign students

Entrance Exams

  • JEE Main (National Level)
  • JEE Advanced (For IITs)
  • State Engineering Exams
  • University-specific entrance tests
