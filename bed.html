What is B.Ed?

Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) is a professional undergraduate degree program that prepares students for a career in teaching. It is a mandatory qualification for those who wish to teach at secondary and higher secondary levels in schools.

The B.Ed program focuses on developing teaching skills, educational psychology, classroom management, and pedagogical knowledge. It combines theoretical knowledge with practical teaching experience through internships and teaching practice sessions.

  • Duration: 2 years (4 semesters)
  • Eligibility: Graduation with 50% marks
  • Recognition: NCTE approved
  • Career Scope: Teaching in schools, education consultancy, curriculum development
  • Specializations: Various subjects based on graduation
  • Practical Training: 16 weeks teaching internship
  • Job Security: Government and private school opportunities
  • Growth Path: Teacher → HOD → Principal → Director
