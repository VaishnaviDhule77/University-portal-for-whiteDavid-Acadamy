Eligibility Criteria

  • For UG: 10+2 from recognized board
  • For PG: Bachelor's degree in relevant field
  • Minimum 45% marks in qualifying exam
  • No age limit for working professionals
  • Relaxation for reserved categories

Documents Required

  • 10th & 12th mark sheets
  • Graduation certificates (for PG)
  • Photo ID proof (Aadhar/PAN)
  • Passport size photographs
  • Address proof
  • Signature scan

Important Dates

  • Admission Open: January & July
  • Last Date to Apply: 30th June / 31st Dec
  • Document Verification: Within 7 days
  • Fee Payment: Within 15 days
  • Classes Commence: 1st Aug / 1st Feb
