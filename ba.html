What is BA (Bachelor of Arts)?

Understanding the Bachelor of Arts Degree Program

Program Overview

BA (Bachelor of Arts) is a 3-year undergraduate degree program focusing on humanities, social sciences, and liberal arts. It develops critical thinking, communication, and analytical skills.

  • Duration: 3 Years (6 Semesters)
  • Level: Undergraduate Degree
  • Field: Arts, Humanities & Social Sciences
  • Mode: Regular/Distance/Online

Key Features

BA program offers diverse specializations with flexible curriculum options and interdisciplinary learning opportunities.

  • Specializations: 20+ Subjects Available
  • Curriculum: Theory + Practical Projects
  • Skills: Communication, Critical Analysis
  • Assessment: Exams + Assignments

BA Eligibility Criteria

Requirements for BA Admission 2024

Academic Requirements

  • 10+2 Qualified: From recognized board
  • Minimum Marks: 45-50% (varies by college)
  • Stream: Any stream (Arts/Science/Commerce)
  • Age Limit: No specific age limit
  • Subjects: English compulsory

Entrance Exams

  • DUET: Delhi University Entrance Test
  • JNUEE: JNU Entrance Exam
  • BHU UET: Banaras Hindu University
  • IPU CET: Guru Gobind Singh University
  • State CET: State-level entrance tests

Reservation Policy

  • SC/ST: 15% & 7.5% reservation
  • OBC: 27% reservation
  • EWS: 10% reservation
  • PWD: 5% reservation
  • Sports: Quota available
