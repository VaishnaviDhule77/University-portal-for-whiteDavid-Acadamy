Admission Process

Simple and Transparent Admission Procedure

Step 1: Choose Program

Browse our programs

Select the program that best suits your career goals and eligibility criteria from our wide range of courses.

Step 2: Fill Application

Online or Offline

Fill the application form with required details and upload necessary documents. Apply online or visit our center.

Step 3: Document Verification

Submit required documents

Submit self-attested copies of required documents for verification. Original documents to be produced if required.

Step 4: Fee Payment

Pay program fee

Pay the program fee through online payment gateway, demand draft, or cash at our admission counter.

Step 5: Confirmation

Receive admission confirmation

Receive your admission confirmation, student ID, and study materials. Start your learning journey!
