Admission Process

Step-by-step guide to secure your seat

1

Research & Selection

Research colleges, check eligibility, and shortlist preferred institutions based on rankings, fees, and location.

2

Application Form

Fill application forms online/offline, upload required documents, and pay application fees before deadline.

3

Entrance Exam

Appear for NCHM JEE or college-specific entrance tests. Prepare for English, Reasoning, GK, and Service Aptitude.

4

GD & PI

Participate in Group Discussion and Personal Interview rounds. Showcase communication skills and hospitality aptitude.

5

Merit List & Counseling

Check merit list, attend counseling sessions, and select college based on rank and seat availability.

6

Fee Payment & Admission

Pay admission fees, submit original documents, complete enrollment formalities, and join orientation program.

Important Dates

  • NCHM JEE Registration: Dec - Feb
  • Entrance Exam: April
  • Result Declaration: May
  • Counseling: June - July
  • Academic Session: August

Entrance Exam Pattern

  • Numerical Ability: 30 questions
  • Reasoning & Logical Deduction: 30 questions
  • General Knowledge: 30 questions
  • English Language: 60 questions
  • Aptitude for Service Sector: 50 questions
  • Total: 200 questions (3 hours)
