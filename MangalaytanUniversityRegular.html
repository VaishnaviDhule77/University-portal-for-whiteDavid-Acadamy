Our Legacy

Established in 2006, Mangalayatan University has emerged as a leading center of excellence in higher education. Located in the historic city of Aligarh, our university is committed to providing quality education that meets global standards.

  • NAAC 'A' Grade Accredited
  • UGC Approved University
  • AICTE Approved Programs
  • ISO 9001:2015 Certified
  • Member of AIU
  • Industry-Academia Collaboration
  • Research & Innovation Focus
  • Holistic Development Approach

Vision

To be a globally recognized university fostering excellence in education, research, and innovation for societal development.

Mission

To provide transformative education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values to become responsible leaders.

Values

Integrity, Excellence, Innovation, Inclusivity, and Social Responsibility form the core of our institutional values.
