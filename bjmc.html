What is BJMC?

Understanding Bachelor of Journalism & Mass Communication

About BJMC Course

Bachelor of Journalism and Mass Communication (BJMC) is a 3-year undergraduate degree program that focuses on developing skills in journalism, media production, advertising, public relations, and digital communication. The course combines theoretical knowledge with practical training to prepare students for dynamic careers in the media industry.

Key Highlights:

  • Duration: 3 Years (6 Semesters)
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with minimum 50-60% marks
  • Age Limit: Usually 17-25 years
  • Admission: Based on entrance exams & merit
  • Course Type: Full-time undergraduate program
  • Average Fee: ₹50,000 - ₹3,00,000 per year

Course Objectives

The BJMC program aims to:

  • Develop strong writing and communication skills
  • Provide understanding of media ethics and laws
  • Train in various forms of journalism (print, broadcast, digital)
  • Build expertise in content creation and media production
  • Prepare for leadership roles in media organizations
  • Foster critical thinking and analytical abilities

BJMC Specializations & Courses

Explore various specializations in Journalism & Mass Communication

Print Journalism

Focus on newspaper and magazine journalism, including news writing, reporting, editing, and publication design. Learn the fundamentals of print media and its evolving role in the digital age.

Broadcast Journalism

Specialize in television and radio journalism, covering news reporting, anchoring, program production, and broadcast management. Gain hands-on experience with modern broadcast equipment.

Digital Journalism

Master online journalism, social media content creation, web publishing, and digital storytelling. Learn SEO, analytics, and multimedia production for online platforms.

Photojournalism

Develop skills in visual storytelling through photography. Learn technical aspects of photography, photo editing, and documentary photography for news and features.

Public Relations

Focus on corporate communication, reputation management, crisis communication, and media relations. Prepare for PR roles in various industries and organizations.

Advertising

Learn advertising principles, copywriting, creative design, media planning, and brand management. Develop skills for creating effective advertising campaigns.

BJMC Eligibility Criteria

Requirements for admission to Bachelor of Journalism & Mass Communication

Educational Qualification

  • 10+2 Completion: From recognized board
  • Minimum Marks: 50-60% aggregate
  • Stream: Any stream (Arts, Commerce, Science)
  • English Proficiency: Good communication skills
  • Age Limit: Usually 17-25 years
  • Appearing: Candidates appearing in 12th can apply

Skills Required

  • Writing Ability: Good command over language
  • Communication: Strong verbal skills
  • Creativity: Innovative thinking ability

  • Observation: Attention to detail
  • Time Management: Ability to meet deadlines
